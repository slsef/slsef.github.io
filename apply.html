Scholarship Overview

The SLSEF Scholarship provides financial assistance of LKR 5,000 per month for one year to deserving Advanced Level (A/L) students with financial difficulties. Based on performance, the scholarship may be extended for another year.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently enrolled in G.C.E. Advanced Level studies in Sri Lanka.
  • Demonstrated financial need.
  • Strong academic record and commitment to education.

Application Requirements

  • Personal information and contact details.
  • Family financial background (household income, occupation, etc.).
  • Latest academic results (O/L or A/L).
  • A personal statement (max 500 words) describing financial need, educational goals, and achievements.
