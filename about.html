Who We Are

The Sri Lanka Student Empowerment Foundation (SLSEF) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to empowering talented Advanced Level (A/L) students from financially challenged backgrounds.

Our Objectives

  • Provide annual scholarships to A/L students demonstrating both talent and need.
  • Encourage academic excellence and extracurricular engagement.
  • Offer mentorship and guidance through our network of volunteers.
  • Foster a culture of giving back â€” where supported students become future mentors and donors.

Core Values

  • Equity: Equal opportunity for every student.
  • Integrity: Fairness and transparency in all processes.
  • Empowerment: Building confidence and self-reliance.
  • Community: Collective growth and collaboration.
