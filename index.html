What SLSEF provides

Financial assistance that helps remove day-to-day barriers to learning.

Mentorship and encouragement from volunteers who want students to progress with confidence.

A transparent selection process with document review, interview, and check-ins after award.

Why SLSEF exists

Talent is widespread. Opportunity is not. The foundation is designed to narrow that gap.

Our Vision

A Sri Lanka where every talented student has equal opportunity to achieve their potential, free from financial and social barriers.

Our Mission

To empower economically disadvantaged yet academically talented students by providing financial assistance, mentorship, and opportunities for personal growth.

How support works

The program is intentionally simple: practical funding, close guidance, and clear accountability.

Scholarship Support

Students receive consistent monthly assistance to reduce the pressure of transport, materials, and core study costs.

Mentorship

Volunteers support students with encouragement, direction, and practical guidance as they move through A/L studies.

Fair Review

Applications move through screening, document checks, and interview steps so awards are transparent and defensible.
