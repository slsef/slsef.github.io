Apply for the SLSEF scholarship.

This scholarship is built for A/L students with strong academic potential and real financial need. Start with the online form, then follow the documented review process.

Open Google Form Review the Process

At a glance

LKR 5,000 per month for one year for eligible students facing financial difficulty.

Support can continue for an additional year when progress and need remain strong.

Shortlisted applicants move through document checks and a remote interview.

Who should apply?

The scholarship is intended for students who are serious about their studies and need meaningful support to continue well.

Eligibility

  • Currently enrolled in G.C.E. Advanced Level studies in Sri Lanka.
  • Demonstrated financial need.
  • Strong academic record and commitment to education.

Prepare These Details

  • Personal information and reliable contact details.
  • Family financial background, including income and occupation.
  • Latest academic results from O/L or A/L studies.
  • A personal statement of up to 500 words explaining need, goals, and achievements.
