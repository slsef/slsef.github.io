About SLSEF

A focused scholarship initiative for students with talent and need.

SLSEF exists to help capable Sri Lankan students continue their A/L studies when financial pressure would otherwise slow them down or push them out.

What defines the foundation

A non-profit structure centered on educational access, not scale for its own sake.

Scholarship support paired with mentorship so students receive both funding and guidance.

A volunteer network committed to fairness, transparency, and long-term student progress.

Who we are

SLSEF is built around a straightforward idea: talented students should not lose momentum because of circumstances they did not choose.

Our Role

The Sri Lanka Student Empowerment Foundation (SLSEF) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to supporting Advanced Level (A/L) students from financially challenged backgrounds.

Our Objectives

  • Provide scholarships to students demonstrating both talent and need.
  • Encourage academic excellence and extracurricular engagement.
  • Offer mentorship and practical guidance through volunteers.
  • Build a culture where supported students become future mentors and donors.

Core values

These principles shape how SLSEF selects students, communicates decisions, and measures its work.

Equity

Every student deserves a fair opportunity to pursue their potential.

Integrity

Selection and communication should remain transparent, consistent, and defensible.

Empowerment

Support should build confidence, independence, and long-term momentum.

Community

Students, volunteers, donors, and partners all contribute to a stronger shared future.
