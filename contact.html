How to reach us

Email is currently the clearest route for all enquiries and allows the team to respond with the right context.

What to Include

Who should contact SLSEF?

The foundation welcomes messages from students, families, schools, donors, volunteers, and organizations that want to support the mission.

  • Students who need help understanding the scholarship process.
  • Donors and supporters exploring how to contribute.
  • Schools and community organizations interested in collaboration.
